PLAY

You never knew it, but it’s easy to improve reliability on your site. Here’s how.

Is easy for play:

  • Make bet with 1 REDLC or 2 REDLC
  • make Roll() function
    • Call Roll() cost 1 REDLC
    • If no call Roll() after 50 bet, Roll() happen automatic
  • Roll() determine by random if ODD or EVEN
  • When ODD, bet of 1 REDLC win
  • When EVEN, bet of 1 REDLC win
  • When lose, you lose bet
  • When win, you win original bet + share of lose pool
  • All onchain. Contract verify. Open source.
